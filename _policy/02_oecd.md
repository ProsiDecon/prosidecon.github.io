I regularly contribute to the OECD's work on Official Development Assistance (ODA) with a specific focus on the Ocean Economy. I contribute to the estimation of headline figures on ODA related to the ocean economy and combatting plastic pollution. I also contribute to maintaining the OECD's Data Platform on Development Finance for the Sustainable Ocean Economy, which I implemented during my time at the OECD.
