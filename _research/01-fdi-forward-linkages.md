---
title: FDI, Forward Linkages, and Services Inputs
pdf_path: /files/papers/fdi-forward-linkages-services-inputs.pdf
---
We provide evidence of spillover effects from foreign direct investment through forward linkages. Using granular information on firm-to-firm transactions and inward FDI in Rwanda, the paper studies persistent effects on domestic firms after sourcing from foreign-owned enterprises, with a focus on value-added, employment, productivity, input quality, and synthetic-control methods for strongly unbalanced panels.
